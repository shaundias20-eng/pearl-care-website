About Pearl Care Training

Trusted by Care Providers Across the UK

Pearl Care Training Academy was founded by experienced care professionals who saw a gap between what traditional training offered and what modern care workers actually needed: flexible, engaging, and truly meaningful education.

We combine deep sector expertise with the latest in AI-powered learning technology to deliver a platform that is as practical on the floor as it is impressive during a CQC inspection.

Our curriculum is authored by registered nurses, social workers, and care managers with decades of frontline and leadership experience in residential care, domiciliary care, and supported living.
